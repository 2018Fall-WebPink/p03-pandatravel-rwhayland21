Why should you visit?

The Cuban Club is in the top ten of most haunted attractions in Florida. The first club burned to the ground in 1916 but they quickley rebuilt a knew one in 1917. The first Cuban Club was a cigar factory that produced many cigars but in the new building it housed a gym, a swimming pool, bowling lanes and a theater. The new club was the place of many deaths. People believed it started to be haunted in 1929 when a young actor took the stage and took his own life. After this actor took his life, many people have died there and people have seen "figures lurking around after hours." If you are looking for a little Halloween getaway or "spook", this place will definetly give it to you!
